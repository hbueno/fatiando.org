Leonardo Uieda

e-mail: leouieda at gmail dot com

I'm a geophysics PhD student working on potential field inversion at the Observatório Nacional, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. I'm currently a member of the IAG Joint Working Group JWG2.8: Modeling and Inversion of Gravity-Solid Earth Coupling. My interests are inverse problems, open source software, and Northern Shaolin Kung Fu.

Research interests

  • Potential field modeling in spherical coordinates
  • 3D potential field inversion
  • Inverse problem methodologies
  • Scientific software development
  • Different ways to incorporate geologic information into geophysical inversion
  • Teaching inverse problems

Education

  • PhD in Geophysics (2011-ongoing): Observatório Nacional, Rio de Janeiro, Brasil
  • MSc in Geophysics (2010-2011): Observatório Nacional, Rio de Janeiro, Brasil
  • International Exchange Program (2008-2009): York University, Toronto, Canada
  • BSc in Geophysics (2009): Universidade de São Paulo, São Paulo, Brasil
